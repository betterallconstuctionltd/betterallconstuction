About us

BETTER ALL CONSTRUCTION COMPANY LTD,We are Rwandan company founded from ideas and initiatives raised since 2015 by graduates and post graduates from higher learning institutions of Rwanda and abroad.

The concern was how to use the skills and knowledge acquired to solve engineering problems in East African region and improve our welfare. One of our country’s (Rwanda) Vision 2020 which is “Building a knowledge based on Technology’’ has also motivated us to develop more of our skills, talents and knowledge through constant studies and researches, hence developing a company with four strong Departments backed by:
1. Civil Engineering
2. Architectural work
However, these skills, talents and knowledge will be transferred to many people in order to increase the productivity and the capacity of the average citizen.
